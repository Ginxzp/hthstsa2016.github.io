Introduction to Engineering Design

New students at High Technology High School are immediately immersed in the vast world of engineering. With such a rigorous and fruitful curriculum, Project Lead The Way provides a myriad of insightful lessons that include CAD modeling, sketch designs, and proper engineering notation. Just channel your inner genius.


With the help of the teacher, students dig deep into the engineering design process, applying math, science, and engineering standards to hands-on projects. It all starts out with simple drawings, which then culminates into 3-D modeling on the Autodesk Inventor Professional software. Projects include the complete replication of a model car with the Autodesk software, in addition to a multitude of different CAD drawings that further aggrandize the aptitude of each student with this complicated software.

Ms. Linda Grunthaner

Linda Grunthaner teaches Introduction to Engineering Design at High Technology High School. She has held the position for 17 years.


"Introduction to Engineering, or IED, is a good introduction for the freshmen because it covers tool use, safety, Beginner to Advanced Autodesk Inventor software, and sketching. It also covers problem solving as a basis to all these design briefs. In my opinion, IED truly paves a road towards an engineering future, as several of my students revisit during or after college to tell me how much IED prepared them in all of their engineering courses."

A FRESH START


When you first walk into High Technology High School as an incoming freshman, you are immediately immersed in a purely engineering environment. With two unique engineering classes, being "Introduction to Engineering Design" and "Computer Integrated Manufacturing", students are given a myriad of opportunities to apply themselves in this field and take off on the journey of finding their inner engineer.

TAKING IT A LEVEL HIGHER


Sophomore year at High Technology High School allows each student to enhance their knowledge of the engineering field, with two more insightful courses: "Principles of Engineering" and "Computer Programming for Engineers." Each course delves into the attributes of mechanincal engineering, programming, and real-world applications of the Project Lead The Way design process.

CHOOSING THE RIGHT PATH


Choosing the right field in engineering is always a hard decision. However, junior year at High Technology High School offers 4 specific technology electives that will fulfill the desire to excel in a specific field or simply help each student realize their place in the vast world of STEM. Consisting of "Computer Science and Software Engineering" and "Civil Engineering and Architecture", our students are completely able to map out their futures.

Senior Year. All the knowledge from 3 years of experience at High Technology High School culminates into the rigorous curriculum of Engineering Design & Development.
