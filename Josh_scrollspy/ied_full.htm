Ms. Linda Grunthaner

Linda Grunthaner teaches Introduction to Engineering Design at High Technology High School. She has held the position for 17 years.
Interview Questions:
​1. Why do you think the course is a good introduction into engineering for the Freshmen?
It covers tool use and safety and Beginner to Advanced Inventor and sketching. It also Covers problem solving as a base.
2.What kinds of skills do the students gain by taking the class?
Proper Tool use, basic drawing & CAD
3.Do students ever revisit you and comment on the relevancy of your course to later classes or the real world of engineering?
Yes they can make basic repairs and solve problems
4. What kinds of projects do the students complete?
Design challenges using the tech lab and Inventor drawings
